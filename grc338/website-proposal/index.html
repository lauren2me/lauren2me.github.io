Introduction

Golden Hour Coffee Company

Golden Hour Coffee Company is a boutique coffee shop located in San Luis Obispo, CA. The space was thoughtfully curated to enhance creativity and inspire artists, dreamers, and go-getters to come in and create while they enjoy delicious coffee drinks. Special events are also hosted every week to encourage community.

Target audience

Our target audience are those who have a deep appreciation for aesthetics. They enjoy the small luxuries in life and consider their morning coffee run to be one of the most important parts of their morning routine. They enjoy building community and support small businesses over chains or large corporations often.

The primary motive of our customer is of course to come in and grab a great coffee. While they are here though, they are also given the opporitunity to stay and work in our comfortable and inviting envioirnemnt and create community with likeminded individuals by atttending our community events.

About Us

Golden Hour Coffee Company was founded in 2022 by photgrapher and designer Lauren Toomey. As an artist, Lauren values aesthetics, community, and a good cup of coffee. Lauren brought these three things together while creating the Golden Hour Coffee Company.
